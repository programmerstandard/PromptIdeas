
## Role And Character Examples
### 1. Person's Values
* **Growth & Learning:** Curiosity, Wisdom, Mastery, Creativity, Open-mindedness
* **Character & Integrity:** Honesty, Authenticity, Courage, Responsibility, Fairness
* **Connection & Others:** Compassion, Family, Loyalty, Kindness, Teamwork
* **Freedom & Lifestyle:** Independence, Adventure, Flexibility, Health, Balance
* **Achievement & Impact:** Ambition, Leadership, Contribution, Excellence, Influence
### 2. Person Attitudes
#### **Positive / Constructive Attitudes**
* **Optimistic:** Expecting favorable outcomes.
* **Resilient:** Bouncing back quickly from setbacks.
* **Open-minded:** Willing to consider new ideas or perspectives.
* **Proactive:** Taking initiative and acting in advance.
* **Grateful:** Regularly appreciating what one has.

#### **Negative / Destructive Attitudes**
* **Pessimistic:** Expecting the worst possible outcome.
* **Cynical:** Distrusting others’ motives; believing people are purely self-interested.
* **Defeatist:** Giving up easily; expecting failure before trying.
* **Apathetic:** Showing little or no interest, enthusiasm, or concern.
* **Entitled:** Believing one inherently deserves special treatment or privileges.

#### **Social / Interpersonal Attitudes**
* **Empathetic:** Understanding and sharing the feelings of others.
* **Cooperative:** Willing to work collaboratively toward a common goal.
* **Judgmental:** Quickly forming critical or negative opinions of others.
* **Competitive:** Strongly driven to be better or win against others.
* **Accommodating:** Willing to adapt, yield, or fit in with others' needs.

#### **Work / Goal-Oriented Attitudes**
* **Diligent:** Careful, persistent, and hardworking.
* **Ambitious:** Having a strong desire and determination to succeed.
* **Complacent:** Self-satisfied and unaware of potential dangers or flaws.
* **Detail-oriented:** Focusing heavily on the specifics and accuracy.
* **Flexible:** Adapting easily to changing conditions or plans.

#### **Self-Oriented Attitudes**
* **Confident:** Trusting in one’s own abilities and judgment.
* **Insecure:** Lacking confidence; uncertain or anxious about oneself.
* **Self-aware:** Conscious of one’s own character, feelings, and impact on others.
* **Egotistical:** Excessively conceited or absorbed in oneself.

### 5. Person Actions

Categories of Common Human Actions

### **Physical Actions**
- Exercising, sleeping, eating, traveling, working
- Caring for body (medical care, hygiene, rest)

### **Mental Actions**
- Learning, reflecting, planning, decision-making
- Problem-solving, creativity, mindfulness

### **Social Actions**
- Communicating, listening, helping, collaborating
- Setting boundaries, apologizing, forgiving

### **Emotional Actions**
- Expressing feelings, regulating emotions, showing empathy
- Processing grief, celebrating joy, managing stress

### **Ethical Actions**
- Being honest, standing up for others, keeping promises
- Doing the right thing even when no one watches
